**Ring of Animal Influence**

Friday, July 27, 2018

3:59 PM

 

<table><tbody><tr class="odd"><td><h1 id="ring-of-animal-influence"><strong>RING OF ANIMAL INFLUENCE</strong></h1><p><em>Ring, rare</em></p><p> </p><p>This ring has 3 charges, and it regains 1d3 expended charges daily at dawn. While wearing the ring, you can use an action to expend 1 of its charges to cast one of the following spells:</p><p> </p><ul><li><blockquote><p>Animal friendship (save DC 13)</p></blockquote></li><li><blockquote><p>Fear (save DC 13), targeting only beasts that have an Intelligence of 3 or lower</p></blockquote></li><li><blockquote><p>Speak with animals</p></blockquote></li></ul></td></tr></tbody></table>
